Our Mission
Three Pillars

TNA exists to shape the future of ninja through goals designed to benefit athletes, fans, and the broader community.

01

Future

Standardizing rules, training programs, and competition formats. Building a structured environment that welcomes new athletes while promoting teamwork and camaraderie.

02

Finance

Athlete financial sustainability through sponsorships, prize money, and diverse revenue. Enabling competitors to focus on training full-time.

03

Fans

An exhilarating viewing experience with high-quality production, dynamic commentary, and interactive audience participation.
