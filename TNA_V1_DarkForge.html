Our Mission

Built on Three Pillars

TNA exists to shape the future of the sport of ninja through goals designed to benefit athletes, fans, and the broader community.

Future

Standardizing rules, training programs, and competition formats. Building a structured environment that welcomes new athletes and promotes teamwork and camaraderie across 24 professional teams.

Finance

Prioritizing athlete financial sustainability through sponsorships, prize money, and diverse revenue opportunities — enabling competitors like Daniel Gil and Joe Moravsky to train full-time.

Fans

Providing an exhilarating viewing experience through high-quality production, dynamic commentary, and interactive audience participation. All events broadcast live on YouTube.
